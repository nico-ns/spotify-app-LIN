A little bit about this app

Basically this app will get your available Spotify data (unfortunately listening history is available only up the previous 50 listened-to songs), analyze it and try to build "coherent" playlists. The analysis and grouping is based on the available audio features from spotify and on sentiment analysis-like algorithms applied to the lyrics (if available). The recommendation system is also based on the your Spotify library, where you will be able to import the generated playlist if you like.
